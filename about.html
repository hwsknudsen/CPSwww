About Us

CPS Distribution Service, in its current vision, traces its roots back to Jon Obers original concept:

To have a warehouse in close proximity to Salem Port, offering storage, originally only frozen.

In more recent times, chilled and dry storage were also added.

The current company and team are now pleased to be able to, once again and on the original site, offer chilled, frozen, and dry storage to the trade in Woodstown and surrounding areas.

Along with storage, we also offer re-packing and other miscellaneous warehouse/storage services.

Please reach out to find out what we might be able to do for you.
