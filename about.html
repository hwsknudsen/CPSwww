About US

CPS Distribution in its current vision traces its roots back the Jon Obers original concept,

To have a warehouse in close proximity to Salem Port, offering storage, Originally only Frozen,

In more recent time chilled and dry storage where also added,

The current company and team are now pleased to be able to again and on the original site,

Offer chilled, frozen and dry storage to the trade in Woodstown and surrounding areas,

Along with storage we also offer re-packing and other miscellaneous warehouse/storage services,
